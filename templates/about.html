Isha l'Isha Haifa Feminist Center

About Us

"Isha l'Isha Haifa Feminist Center" is a vibrant community of women dedicated to promoting gender equality and empowering women from all walks of life. Founded in 1983 by passionate feminist activists in Haifa, our center has been a leading voice in the struggle for women's rights in Israel for decades.

Our mission is to advance the status and rights of women and girls, fostering peace, security, and socio-economic justice from a feminist perspective. We believe in eradicating all forms of discrimination, violence, and oppression against women.

At "Isha l'Isha," we work tirelessly to protect women's rights to make decisions about their bodies, and we actively engage in creating an environment of understanding, dialogue, and commonality among women from diverse cultures and backgrounds.

Our Vision

We envision a society where all women enjoy equal rights in all fields. A society free from gender-based violence, where women's voices are heard and respected. We strive to create a society in which women have full and equal access to all opportunities and resources.

Our Goals

  • To eradicate every form of discrimination, violence, and oppression against women.
  • To protect our rights to make decisions about our bodies.
  • To create the conditions for understanding, commonality, and dialogue between women of different cultures and backgrounds.
  • To improve existing services and adapt them to the specific needs of women.
  • Social change by raising awareness of issues of equality.
  • To take action against any sexist expression in society and to spread the idea of equal rights and opportunities for all women.
