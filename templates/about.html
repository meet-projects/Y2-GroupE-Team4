Isha l'Isha Haifa Feminist Center

About Us

Isha l'Isha is a feminist community in Haifa, Israel. Founded in 1983, our mission is to advance women's rights and promote peace, security, and socio-economic justice.

Our Goals:

  • Eradicate discrimination, violence, and oppression against women.
  • Protect women's rights to make decisions about their bodies.
  • Create understanding and dialogue among women of different cultures and backgrounds.
  • Improve services to meet the specific needs of women.
  • Raise awareness about issues of equality and take action against sexism in society.

Our Vision:

A society where all women enjoy equal rights in all fields, free from gender-based violence, and their voices are heard.

Join us in our mission to empower women and promote gender equality.
